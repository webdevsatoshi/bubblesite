Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how AvaTexts ("AvaTexts," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use the AvaTexts website (avatexts.com), the Ava AI companion messaging service, and any related services (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Service.

1. Definitions

  • Personal Data: Any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
  • Service: The AvaTexts website, the Ava AI companion, and all related services and interfaces.
  • Usage Data: Data collected automatically through use of the Service (e.g., page visits, device information).
  • You: The individual using the Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Phone Number: Your phone number serves as your primary account identifier and is required to access the Service through iMessage.
  • Message Content: The messages you send to and receive from Ava, including any personal information, preferences, or other details you share during conversations.
  • Payment Information: When you subscribe to a paid plan, our third-party payment processor (Stripe) collects your payment card information, billing address, and other financial details. We do not directly store your full payment card information. Stripe's use of your data is governed by their privacy policy.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website or use the Service, we may automatically collect:

  • Device and Network Data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked, referring URLs, and other browsing behavior on our website.
  • Analytics Data: We use third-party analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our website. These tools may use cookies, pixels, or similar technologies to collect aggregated and anonymized usage information.

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience and analyze site traffic. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, though some features of the website may not function properly without them.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing the Service: To deliver, operate, and maintain the Ava AI companion experience, including generating personalized responses and maintaining conversation context and memory.
  • Account Management: To identify you, manage your subscription, and provide customer support.
  • Payment Processing: To process subscription payments and manage billing through our third-party payment processor.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage trends, diagnose technical issues, and improve the quality and performance of the Service.
  • Communications: To send you service-related messages, including subscription confirmations, billing notifications, and important updates about the Service.
  • Safety and Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or government requests.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your Personal Data. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • AI Service Providers: To generate Ava's responses, portions of your message content are processed by third-party AI language model providers. These providers process data on our behalf to deliver the Service and are contractually prohibited from using your data for their own purposes, including training their models on your conversations.
  • Payment Processor: We share necessary billing information with Stripe to process your subscription payments. Stripe's handling of your data is governed by their own privacy policy.
  • Analytics Providers: We share anonymized and aggregated usage data with third-party analytics providers to help us understand website traffic and usage patterns.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect our rights or safety, prevent fraud, or respond to a government request.
  • Business Transfers: If AvaTexts is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your Personal Data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, including:

  • Message Content: Conversation data is retained to maintain Ava's memory and provide a continuous, personalized experience. If you request deletion of your data, we will remove your conversation history within 30 days.
  • Account Information: Your phone number and account data are retained for the duration of your use of the Service and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.
  • Payment Records: Transaction records are retained as required by applicable financial regulations and tax laws.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely for aggregate analysis.

6. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or destruction. These measures include encryption of data in transit and at rest, access controls, and secure infrastructure.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and phone number.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your Personal Data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the Personal Data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate Personal Data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your Personal Data. To request complete deletion of your data, contact us at support@avatexts.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days, subject to any legal retention obligations.
  • Opt-Out of Communications: You may opt out of promotional messages at any time by texting STOP.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@avatexts.com.

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • The right to know what Personal Data we collect, use, and disclose.
  • The right to request deletion of your Personal Data.
  • The right to opt out of the sale of your Personal Data. We do not sell your Personal Data.
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@avatexts.com.

9. International Users

Your information may be processed and stored in the United States, where our servers and service providers are located. By using the Service, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in the United States. If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that data protection laws in the United States may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect Personal Data from individuals under 18. If we become aware that we have collected data from a person under 18, we will promptly delete it. If you believe a minor has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us at support@avatexts.com.
